Tense Rule Example
Present Perfect Continuous Subject + has/have + been + verb(-ing) I have been studying for two hours.
Past Perfect Continuous Subject + had + been + verb(-ing) They had been waiting for an hour when the bus arrived.
Future Perfect Continuous Subject + will + have + been + verb(-ing) By next month, we will have been living here for a year.
